Behavioral Notes & Test Criteria

Behavior: Income calculation updates in real-time as the host adjusts their schedule or pricing. The rent coverage bar animates smoothly (300ms ease-out) when values change. Tapping the income amount reveals a monthly breakdown. The component appears immediately after schedule entry in the listing wizard — no intermediate steps between 'when am I available?' and 'how much will I earn?'
